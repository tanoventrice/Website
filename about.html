About XRP, XLM, and SOL

XRP (Ripple)

XRP is a digital asset and cryptocurrency designed for fast, low-cost international payments. It is the native token of the XRP Ledger, developed by Ripple Labs.

XLM (Stellar Lumens)

XLM is the native cryptocurrency of the Stellar network, which aims to facilitate cross-border payments and connect financial institutions, payment systems, and people.

SOL (Solana)

SOL is the native token of the Solana blockchain, known for its high throughput and low transaction fees. Solana supports fast and scalable decentralized applications.
