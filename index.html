What is XRP?

XRP is a digital asset developed by Ripple Labs for fast, low-cost international payments. It acts as a bridge currency in cross-border transfers and is optimized for institutional use.

What is XLM?

Stellar (XLM) is an open-source network that facilitates low-cost, cross-border payments. It's designed for global financial access and was created by the Stellar Development Foundation.

What is SOL?

Solana (SOL) is a fast, scalable blockchain built for decentralized apps, NFTs, and DeFi. Known for low fees and high throughput, Solana supports thousands of transactions per second.
